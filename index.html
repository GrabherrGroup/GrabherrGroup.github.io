Grabherr Group Software

Advancing Research in Computational Biology and Medicine

Below is a list of software developed and maintained by the Grabherr Group:

Ananas : A de novo assembler of RNA-Sequence data

Smörgås : An Ultra Fast Aligner for Protein Sequences

Kraken : A universal genomic coordinate translator for comparative genomics

Saguaro : An unsupervised population genomics analysis program

Whiteboard : A programmatic interface for the visualization of complex biological data

Satsuma : A Highly sensitive whole-genome synteny aligner

Cola : A modular and Configurable Optimal Sequence Aligner
